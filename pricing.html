Frequently Asked Questions

What is the main different between the Pro and ZIP Code plans?

Our Pro and ZIP Code plans include all our social media marketing tools. ZIP Code plans take it one step further and allow you to represent an area and generate leads in that ZIP.

Can I still buy ads if I'm on a Pro Plan?

Yes! All PropertySimple plans have ad capabilities.

How does the free trial work?

You will have access to all PropertySimple Pro features for 7 days. You can cancel anytime with no penalty before the trial is up. If you cancel, you will lose access to Pro features and you will not be charged.

What if I want to cancel?

Great online marketing takes time to build, that's why we ask clients for a 6-month commitment. If you are unsatisfied after 6 months you can cancel, no questions asked. If you decide to cancel early, you will be responsible for a cancellation fee.
