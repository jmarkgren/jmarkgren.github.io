Alice Through the Looking-Glass

When I read Alice Through the Looking-Glass, I got lost in Lewis Carroll's Looking-Glass world and its alternate reality. This fantasy place with rules of its own inspired me to create a typographic book cover using reflected words and a faint mirror outline behind them. The mischievous theme is continued inside the book with full spreads of playful quotes that break up the content of the story.

Below I have created a poster that represents my favorite character, the White Queen. The White Queen gives wise advice to Alice but talks in riddles and believes that time happens backwards so Alice doesn't always understand what the White Queen is trying to tell her.

El mundo de fantasía de Lewis Carroll me inspiró a crear una portada de libro tipográfica utilizando palabras reflejadas y un espejo de tenue contorno detrás de ellos. El tema, de forma traviesa, continúa en el interior del libro con las diferentes de citas lúdicas que rompen el contenido de la historia.

A continuación, he creado un afiche que representa mi personaje favorito; la Reina Blanca. La Reina Blanca da un sabio consejo a Alice, pero habla con enigmas y cree que el tiempo corre hacia atrás, por lo que Alice no siempre entiende lo que la Reina Blanca le está tratando de decirle.
